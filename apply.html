Rental Criteria

Applicants may be evaluated based on the following:

  • Gross monthly income of at least 3 times the monthly rent.
  • Credit history and overall debt obligations.
  • Employment stability and income verification.
  • Rental history and landlord references.
  • Eviction history.
  • Criminal background check, where permitted by law.
  • Number of occupants and compliance with occupancy standards.
  • Pet information, if applicable.

Required Documents

  • Government-issued photo ID.
  • Proof of income, such as recent pay stubs, offer letter, or tax documents.
  • Employment verification.
  • Previous landlord contact information.
  • Pet records, if applicable.

Terms and Conditions

By submitting an application, the applicant confirms that all information provided is true, complete, and accurate to the best of their knowledge.

Submission of an application does not guarantee approval or create a lease agreement. A lease is not valid until it is reviewed, signed by all required parties, and all required move-in funds have been paid and cleared.

The property owner reserves the right to verify employment, income, rental history, references, credit history, background information, and eviction records as part of the application review process.

False, incomplete, or misleading information may result in denial of the application or termination of the lease if discovered later.

Application and screening fees are paid directly to the third-party screening provider and may be non-refundable.

All applicants will be considered in compliance with applicable federal, state, and local fair housing laws.
